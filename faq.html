Everything you need to know about Fuel STK and how it can transform your nutrition.

What is Fuel STK?−

Fuel STK is an AI-powered meal planning app that builds meal plans from the groceries you already have. Tell it your goals, stock your pantry, and it creates meals designed to hit your macros — no spreadsheets or guesswork required.

How does the AI meal planning work?+

Fuel STK uses Claude to choose a coherent, realistic set of foods for each meal from your pantry, then a math solver calculates the exact grams needed to hit your macro targets — so the food choices are sensible and the numbers are always precise.

What's the difference between a regular plan and an AI plan?+

Your plan generates automatically from your pantry and targets using the solver, which locks in exact macros every time. Tapping the "✨ AI plan" button additionally uses Claude to pick more coherent, realistic food combinations — the solver still sets the exact grams either way. AI generations are limited per day; the regular plan is unlimited, and you get one free AI generation the first time you build a plan.

Do I have to track calories or macros manually?+

No — Fuel STK calculates your daily targets and builds meal plans to match them automatically. You can also log food manually any time you want to track something outside your plan.

What if I have dietary restrictions or food preferences?+

Fuel STK doesn't currently track allergies or dietary restrictions, so it's important to review any suggested meal before eating it. This is on our roadmap — for now, simply avoid adding foods to your pantry that you can't or don't want to eat.

How do I add my groceries?+

Scan a barcode or search by name — Fuel STK looks up nutrition info automatically and adds it to your pantry. You can also enter a food manually if it isn't in the database.

Can I mark a food as out of stock without deleting it?+

Yes — toggle a food's stock status right from the Pantry tab. Out-of-stock foods stay in your pantry (so anything you've already logged is unaffected) but won't be used in new meal plans until you mark them back in stock.

Can I edit my meal plan?+

Yes. You can swap ingredients, add or remove foods, and adjust quantities on any meal — Fuel STK will recalculate portions to help keep your day on target.

How do I swap a food in my meal plan?+

Tap the swap icon on any item in a meal, then pick a replacement from your pantry. Fuel STK tries to match the macros of the food you're swapping out, so a swap doesn't throw off what that meal was already hitting.

How do I set up my meal schedule?+

From the Plan tab, tap the meal schedule banner to open the editor. There you can choose how many meals and snacks you eat each day, adjust how much of your daily macros each one should carry, and pin a specific favorite meal to any slot so it shows up automatically.

Can I add an extra meal just for one day?+

Yes — tap "Add a meal for today" on the Plan tab to add a one-off meal without changing your regular schedule. Once you've added food to it, Fuel STK can offer to recalculate your other meals to make room — it will never touch a meal you've already logged.

How do I log what I actually ate?+

Tap "I ate this" on any meal in your plan to log it in one tap, or add food manually from the Log tab. Once a meal is logged, Fuel STK never changes its portions again — even if you edit or recalculate the rest of the day.

Can I save a meal as a favorite to reuse later?+

Yes — tap "Save as favorite" on any meal in your plan. You can pull a favorite back into a future plan, or pin it to a specific slot in your meal schedule so it always shows up there.

Is Fuel STK suitable for everyone?+

Fuel STK provides general nutrition and meal-planning information — it's not a substitute for medical or dietary advice from a qualified professional. If you have a medical condition or specific health needs, check with a doctor or registered dietitian before making significant changes to your diet.

Is my data secure?+

Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit, and our database uses row-level security so your information is only ever accessible from your own account. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.

What are the pricing and subscription options?+

Fuel STK is currently free during early access. We'll announce pricing details before any paid tier launches — early access users will be the first to know.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?+

There's no subscription yet — everything is free right now. When a paid tier does launch, cancelling will be simple and immediate, no hoops to jump through.
